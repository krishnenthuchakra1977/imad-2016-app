Arnold Classic Asia 2016

Arnold Classic Asia 2016 will he held on 19th, 20th and 21st August 2016 in Hong Kong. This is the last Arnold classic of the season before the Mr Olympia 2016 event. There are just 4 weeks after this event for Mr Olympia , which makes this event very important. Here is the last chance for pro athletes to qualify for Mr Olympia. Both Men’s bodybuilding and Women’s fitness athletes who win at Arnold classic with automatically qualify for Mr Olympia in September.
